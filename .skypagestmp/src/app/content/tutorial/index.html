The stache-tutorial tag is a wrapper that holds the rest of the information (heading, summary, and steps) for the tutorial on your page.

Before you get to your steps, we recommend you provide a tutorial title and summary of what the user should expect to learn.

<stache-tutorial> <stache-tutorial-heading> This optional tag is the title for your Turtorial </stache-tutorial-heading> <stache-tutorial-summary> This optional tag is the summary for the following tutorial </stache-tutorial-summary> </stache-tutorial>

The stache-tutorial-step tag is used for each step in your tutorial. It has the optional property showNumber. This allows you to decide if you want your tutorial step to have a number associated with it (defaults to true).

<stache-tutorial-step showNumber="false"> <stache-tutorial-step-heading> This optional tag is the steps title. </stache-tutorial-step-heading> <stache-tutorial-step-body> This tag should wrap your content for the tutorial step. </stache-tutorial-step-body> </stache-tutorial-step>
