To update Stache follow the guides set out in the SKY UX Migration Guide. The migration plugin from SKY UX will replace deprecated Stache components, reoragnize your imported modules, and upgrade your site to SKY UX 3 and Stache 3.

In the migration from Stache v2.18.0, many components were deprecated and replaced by Sky equivalents. As part of the above migration, the following stache components are replaced for you.

  • stache-code-block — sky-code-block
  • stache-code — sky-code
  • stache-image — sky-image
  • stache-hero — sky-hero
  • stache-hero-heading — sky-hero-heading
  • stache-hero-subheading — sky-hero-subheading
  • stache-video — sky-video
  • stache-grid — sky-grid
  • stache-row — sky-row
  • stache-column — sky-column
  • stache-internal — sky-restricted-view
