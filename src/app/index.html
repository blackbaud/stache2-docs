Why Stache?

Stache enables you to create documentation websites optimized for consumers. Stache is a SKY UX SPA with additional documentation-orientated features. With the power of Stache and SKY UX, your site will have a consistent user experience and strong information architecture that's accessible from anywhere on any device.

Best of all, you don't need to create your own components or learn Angular; you can start adding content in Markdown or HTML. With our pre-defined options for styling content, you can grab and go!

Stache for Blackbaud users

For Blackbaud users, additional features are available to help you meet Blackbaud standards.

  • To verify internal content is accessible to Blackbaud employees only, Stache uses the Blackbaud Identity Service for authentication.

  • To enable search for your site and include your content in the Blackbaud global search , we provide the Stache Search Service.

  • Blackbaud Stache SPAs use Git in VSTS. Blackbaud VSTS repos are set up with branch policies that encourage peer review and a build process for ensuring your content can deploy successfully to Azure. This means you can have more confidence that your consumers have the most accurate information with no website downtime.
