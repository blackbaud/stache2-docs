Why Stache?

A First-Class Experience for Consumers

Building off the world-class styles and components of SKY UX, Stache makes your technical documentation easy to use. With built-in templates and components created especially for documentation, your content will be designed with your audience’s needs in mind.

If you're a Blackbaud user, we have additional features available to meet Blackbaud standards. Stache uses the Blackbaud Identity Service for quick authentication. And while Stache helps you build strong information architecture, we have a Stache Search Service that’s built specifically for Blackbaud SKY UX SPAs.

With these features and more, your consumers can focus more on learning and less on finding the information they need to succeed.


Spend Time on What Matters Most

When you need to create technical documentation, Stache removes all the tedious time-consuming tasks off your plate so you can focus on what matters most. With a few simple commands, you can have a new website set up and ready for you to add content in minutes. Instead of spending time styling, creating components, or learning Angular, you can get started adding content in markdown or HTML. With our pre-defined options for styling content, you can grab and go! And because Stache is open source, if you do have a custom requirement you can build upon the Stache foundation.

For Blackbaud users, because Stache is a component library for SKY UX, you also get the benefits of SKY UX. Quick set up with the SKY UX App Management Portal. Your Stache SPA comes with Azure server storage and automatically configured deployments. With the power of SKY UX + Stache, you can go from concept to customer-ready website in no time!
